Common Questions

Is this free or does it cost me anything?

No. Mortgage advice is paid for by the lender if you proceed. There is no cost to review your options or discuss strategy.

Why not just renew with my bank?

Banks are not required to offer their best rate or structure. A review compares your renewal against real alternatives so you can decide with clarity.

Will checking options affect my credit score?

No. Strategy discussions and scenario reviews do not impact your credit. A credit check only happens if you choose to proceed.

What if refinancing does not make sense for me?

Then you are told exactly that. Sometimes the best move is to stay put. The goal is the right decision, not a transaction.

How long does the review take?

Most reviews take about 15 minutes once your details are submitted.
